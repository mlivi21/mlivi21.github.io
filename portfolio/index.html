Potato

Matt Livingston is a student working toward a career in graphic design and who will be graduating with his graphic design and web development diploma in December 2022. From there he hopes to do freelance design work while looking for a design/tech job.

In his younger years, Matt participated in various sports, especially go kart racing and soccer, where he learned quick, calm thinking and leadership skills. Matt has always had a passion for auto racing because he was a mesmerized by the speed of the cars and bravery of the drivers. One day, he hopes to design helmets and other gear for drivers and teams because it ties his passion for racing with his creativity and skills in graphic design.
